About Us

Aloha! Briana’s Boppin Burgers is a food truck located in Honolulu, Hawaii. We established in 2007. We sell burgers and fries. And the drinks are super refreshing. You can usually catch us when you are done surfing at the beach. The cool thing about our truck is when you buy a burger, you get a lei!! That’s boppin. So if you're hungry after you ripped up some waves. Just hop on over to Briana’s Boppin’ Burgers!
